Palma de Mallorca, Spain
⚓ Docked 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
▾
Getting to the City
  • Ship shuttle (free/low-cost) from terminal — check Daily Planner the night before
  • Taxi from terminal to Old Town: ~8–12 EUR, 5–10 min
  • Walk: 1.5 km along scenic waterfront promenade (~20 min)
Top Sights
  • La Seu Cathedral — Gothic masterpiece on the waterfront. Arrive before 10 AM to beat tour groups
  • Palau de l'Almudaina — Royal Palace next to the cathedral
  • Arab Baths (Banys Arabs) — unique preserved Moorish baths
  • Mercat de l'Olivar — indoor food market, great for an authentic cheap lunch
  • Passeig des Born — main boulevard, outdoor cafes
  • Es Baluard Museum — modern art with stunning clifftop terrace
Where to Eat
  • Mercat de l'Olivar stalls — fresh seafood, tapas, local wine
  • Bar Espanya (Placa de la Reina) — outdoor terrace, classic tapas
  • Patisseria Forn des Teatre — local bakery for ensaimadas pastries
Suggested Flow: La Seu when it opens (10 AM) → 1 hour → Mercat de l'Olivar for lunch (11:30 AM) → Old Town on foot → back to ship by 3:30 PM.

Provence (Marseille), France
⚓ Docked 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM
▾
Getting to the City
  • Free port shuttle: Follow the Blue Line on the pier — check Daily Planner for timing
  • Taxi to Vieux-Port: ~10–15 EUR, 10–15 min
  • Petit Train Touristique: Goes up to Notre-Dame de la Garde (~6 EUR round trip)
Top Sights
  • Vieux-Port (Old Port) — heart of Marseille, fish market, locals, cafes
  • MuCEM — stunning modern museum connected to old fort by a footbridge
  • Notre-Dame de la Garde — hilltop basilica with 360° views. Take the Petit Train up
  • Le Panier district — oldest neighborhood, colorful streets and street art
  • Chateau d'If — island fortress (Count of Monte Cristo). Ferry from Vieux-Port ~15 min. Confirm time permits
Where to Eat
  • Fish stalls at Quai des Belges — fresh sea urchins and grilled fish right off the boat
  • Le Comptoir Dugommier — casual lunch near Vieux-Port
  • La Caravelle rooftop bar — overlooking Vieux-Port, perfect pre-ship aperitif
  • Navette biscuits — traditional orange-blossom cookies to take home
Time is tighter here (6.5 hours). Focus on Vieux-Port + MuCEM + one of: Notre-Dame OR Chateau d'If. Not both. Allow 30 min to return to the terminal.

Fri, Jun 5
Naples / Capri, Italy
⚓ Docked 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM
▾
Most walkable port of the trip! The cruise terminal is inside the city — walk straight off the gangway into Naples. No shuttle or bus required.
Suggested Day Plan
  • 7:30 AM: Gangway opens — walk directly to Spaccanapoli (~15 min)
  • 8:00–10:00 AM: Walk Spaccanapoli — churches, bakeries, street life, Gesu Nuovo church, Santa Chiara cloister
  • 10:00 AM: Piazza del Plebiscito — Royal Palace, Basilica, grand square
  • 11:00 AM: Pizza at L'Antica Pizzeria da Michele or Sorbillo — arrive BEFORE noon to avoid the queue. Only Margherita or Marinara at da Michele (~6–8 EUR). Cash only.
  • 12:30 PM: Castel dell'Ovo — FREE entry, excellent harbour views
  • 2:00 PM: National Archaeological Museum (optional) — world-class Pompeii artifacts
  • 4:00 PM: Begin heading back toward terminal along Via Partenope
  • Pompeii option: Circumvesuviana train from Napoli Centrale (~30 min, ~3 EUR each way) — allow 4–5 hours total
Food & Shopping
  • L'Antica Pizzeria da Michele (Via Cesare Sersale 1) — most famous pizzeria in Naples
  • Pizzeria Gino Sorbillo (Via dei Tribunali 32) — great alternative
  • Sfogliatelle pastries — flaky shell filled with ricotta. Pasticceria Pintauro on Via Toledo
  • Sansevero Chapel — the 'Veiled Christ' marble sculpture. Book online
  • Limoncello — this region is the source. Buy a bottle to bring home (checked luggage, wrapped)
  • Shopping: Via Toledo is Naples' main shopping street
⚠️ Safety note: Naples has a reputation for petty crime. Keep bags zipped, phones in front pockets, wallets in interior jacket pockets. Millions visit safely each year — just stay aware.

Connectivity
📱 T-Mobile in Europe

Free international data included (2G speeds — good for maps, texts, email). International calls $0.25/min — use WhatsApp instead. Add International Day Pass ($5/day/line) on port days for full LTE speed.

🚢 At Sea — IMPORTANT!

T-Mobile does NOT cover ships at sea. Turn on Airplane Mode the moment the ship leaves port, then re-enable Wi-Fi only. Maritime roaming can cost $2.50–$5.00/MB — do not let your phone connect automatically.

📶 Ship Wi-Fi (VOOM)

VOOM Surf: basic browsing/email. VOOM Surf+Stream: video calls and streaming. Purchase before the cruise via the Royal Caribbean app for 20–30% off onboard pricing. The RC app works FREE on the ship's internal network for schedules, menus, and messaging the group.
